ATORAN Billing System

Roles

UX/UI Designer
Graphic Designer

Description

The e-SeQR is a record authentication system designed to counter the spread of fake records and documents, such as Diplomas, and Certificates. In this project, I handled UX and graphic design, focusing on logo creation and UX design tasks.

By merging UX and graphic design principles, I delivered a digital experience that seamlessly blends functionality with aesthetic appeal. Additionally, I made minor UI enhancements to refine and improve the system's user interface developed by the developer team.
